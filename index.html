Atom Mapping Generation#

Atom mappings were generated with the Reaction Decoder Tool (ðŸ”— RDT), due to the large quantity of reactions available. RDT is open-source, which makes it easy to modify and integrate in existing pipelines. RDT generates atom mappings in the form of RXN Files from unmapped MOL files. A disadvantage of RDT is that only about 90% of atom mappings are correct (with some enzyme categories being better than others).
